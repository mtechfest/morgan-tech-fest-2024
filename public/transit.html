Ways to get here

Plan Your Trip

Pick the option that fits your journey. The “Get directions” button above will route you from wherever you are.

By car & parking

Morgan TechFest is in Northeast Baltimore at Hillen Road & Cold Spring Lane. From I-95 or I-83, follow signs toward Morgan State University. Set your GPS to 4200 Hillen Road. Visitor parking is available on the West Campus near the building.

MTA bus

The MTA CityLink Silver stops right at campus (Hillen Rd & Cold Spring Ln), and LocalLink 28 runs along Cold Spring Lane. Pay with the CharmPass app or exact fare, and use the MTA trip planner to route from your stop.

By train (Amtrak / MARC)

Take Amtrak (Northeast Regional) or the MARC Penn Line to Baltimore Penn Station, then board the CityLink Silver bus (Charles St & Penn Station) north to Hillen Rd & Cold Spring Ln, a short ride from the venue.

From BWI Airport

From BWI Thurgood Marshall Airport, take the Light RailLink to Penn Station, then the CityLink Silver bus to campus. By car or rideshare it is roughly a 30–40 minute drive.

From Washington, DC

Take the MARC Penn Line or Amtrak from Union Station to Baltimore Penn Station, then the CityLink Silver bus to campus (about 1.5 hours total). Driving is roughly one hour via the BW Parkway or I-95.

Rideshare & taxi

Uber, Lyft, or a taxi will bring you straight to the door. Set your destination to “Earl G. Graves School of Business, Morgan State University” or 4200 Hillen Road, Baltimore, MD 21218.

On the day: doors open at 9:00 AM on September 19, 2026. Give yourself extra time for parking and check-in, and head for the Earl G. Graves School of Business on the West Campus.
